Rules

Gameplay Rules

Redstone and Farms

  • If either redstone or farm causes lag make it less complex
  • Redstone must have a on/off switch
  • Make sure to avoid large hopper chains. Use water streams and bubble columns where possible
  • Lock unused hoppers by placing a full block over the hopper
  • Farms must have overflow protection, to destroy excess items.
  • Limit farms to 100 entities per farm
  • Chunk suppression and mob switches are NOT allowed

Glitch/Bugs/Dupes

  • Any undecided glitch/dupe will be voted by the community
  • ONLY TNT duping is allowed for the time being

Mark player base

  • Use a visible sign or bookstand with your username to signify that it is your base
  • Make sure to claim your base by posting the coordinates in the #share-builds channel to avoid claims issues
  • No building within 100 blocks of another base without consent

Griefing

  • No griefing unless explicit confirmation from the owner that they are not coming back to the server/ not using thatbase anymore
  • When players leave the server their base/builds are considered abandoned
  • There are no roll-backs (Fire-Tick is on)

Social Rules

Treat everyone equally

  • Do not discriminate against anyone in any way, shape or form

PvP

  • If both parties agree, then PvP is allowed

Pranking

  • Do it in moderation
  • Do not be a dick
  • Do it responsibly (This includes PvP and PvE engagement too)
  • Consult with Moderation Team if unsure

Offensive/NSFW Content

  • Only allowed in #pillow-talk
  • Allowed in moderation, be mindful of others and their views

Advertisement

  • Ask the mods before advertising
  • No Twitch advertising without asking mods
  • Deliberately advertising is not allowed
  • If allowed to advertise do in moderation

Appropriate username/skin

  • Players with inappropriate usernames will be asked to change their username
  • Players with inappropriate skins will be asked to change their skin
  • Players are encouraged to nickname themselves in Discord to their Minecraft IGN or closely related to their IGN

Visiting another member's base

  • Do not grief/steal
  • Leave if owner thinks the visit is unwelcomed

Threats

  • Any type of threat such as DDOS, Blackmail, Hacking, Bullying will not be tolerated
  • File a complaint or dm any of the Family Managers if needed

Drama

  • Any drama on the server will result in a removal from the community
  • Take it to DM if necessary

Controversial Topics

  • This includes Political, Religious, Gender Rights, etc must be discussed in private dm/group chat

Multiple Accounts

  • The player who is sharing an account with their family or friends must consult with the Moderation Team, before joining the server

Lying

  • Lying will not be tolerated and result in removal from the community
  • False accusation of other player's actions will result in removal from the community

Game Modification

  • QoL client-side mods are allowed
  • Any mod that gives the player an unfair advantage over other players are not allowed
  • Ask Family Managers if unsure (See also Recommended Mods)

Majority Vote

  • If the majority decided that a certain player is not deemed fit to be part of the community, they will be removed
  • This can be done by starting a poll or by filing a complaint
